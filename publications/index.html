Automorphism Groups of Elliptic Curves with j-Invariant Zero

L. Kriz

Research Notes (2026) [PDF]

A rigorous treatment proving |Aut(E,O)| = 6 for elliptic curves with j = 0 over algebraically closed fields of characteristic > 3. Includes explicit construction of all six automorphisms, proof that no others exist, global classification showing no j with |Aut| > 6, and the connection between automorphisms and twist structure (quadratic, cubic, sextic twists).
